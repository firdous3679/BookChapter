Precision Medicine with AI in Oncology

Innovative Approaches to Cancer Detection and Treatment

About the Book

"Precision Medicine with AI in Oncology - Innovative Approaches to Cancer Detection and Treatment" is an upcoming book seeking contributions from researchers, clinicians, and experts in the field of artificial intelligence and oncology.

This book aims to provide a comprehensive exploration of how AI is reshaping cancer detection and treatment, covering fundamental concepts, applications in early detection, predictive models, personalized treatment, ethical considerations, and real-world case studies. The scope of this publication includes, but is not limited to:

  • AI in Oncology
  • Data Acquisition and Processing in Oncology
  • AI Algorithms and Models for Cancer Diagnosis
  • Imaging Analysis for Early Cancer Detection
  • Predictive Modeling for Cancer Risk Assessment
  • Biomarkers and Genetic Analysis in Early Detection
  • AI-Driven Predictive Models for Cancer Prognosis
  • Personalized Treatment Planning in Oncology
  • Drug Discovery and Targeted Therapies
  • Patient Privacy and Data Security in AI-Enabled Oncology
  • Policy, Transparency, and Informed Consent
  • Regulatory Frameworks for AI in Healthcare
  • Impact of AI on the Healthcare Ecosystem
  • Emerging Trends and Future Innovations in AI-Driven Oncology

The book is edited by Dr. Danish Jamil, Dr. Shehnilla Zardari, Dr. Firdous Kausar, and Dr. Khalid Hussain and will be published by Springer Nature Switzerland AG.
